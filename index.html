Travel to the Basque Country!



  • Also known as Eusaki in the Basque Language (which is considered difficult to learn)
  • Not really a country, but rather a community tied together by their language and culture.
  • The Basques are known as one of the oldest races in Europe, predating the Romans.
  • Is made up of a total of 7 provinces.
    • 3 are found in Spain
      • Vizcaya
      • Álava
      • Guipúzcoa
    • 3 are found in France
      • Basse - Navarre
      • Labourd
      • Soule
