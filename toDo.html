Things To Do in the Basque Country

In the Basque Country there are plenty of activites to try, and places to visit. Each can offer a unique experience, and take you deeper in to the lives and culture of the Basque people.

Guggenheim Museum at Bilbao

Committed to innovation, the Solomon R. Guggenheim Foundation collects, preserves, and interprets modern and contemporary art, and explores ideas across cultures through dynamic curatorial and educational initiatives and collaborations. With its constellation of architecturally and culturally distinct museums, exhibitions, publications, and digital platforms, the foundation engages both local and global audiences.

- Guggenheim Museum

Barrika Beach

Located in Barrika, the 600 metres-long beach of the same name is a wild area of rocks and sand surrounded by spectacular cliffs. It is 25km from Bilbao. Made of stones and sand, some stairs facilitate the access to this beautiful sandy area which is open to the sea. Due to its features, it is very frequented by surfers and nudists.

- Tourism Euskadi

Gorbea Natural Park

The Gorbea Natural Park can be found in the Basque Country, and has many differing features like the Itxina Protected Biotope and the Gujuli Waterfall. It is sutable for most ages ranging from children to adults, and is great for hiking or other similar outdoor activities.

Tourism Euskadi

San Sebastian

Among the many things that can be done in the city of San Sebastian, there is the Old Town. It is a very social portion of the city, and many different activities ranging from going out for delicious foods, to admiring the architecture. This is perfect for families and tourists, where they can get to explore the city and visit the shops that are available.
